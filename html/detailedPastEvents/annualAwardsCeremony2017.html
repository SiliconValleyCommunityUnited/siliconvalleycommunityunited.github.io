In the past year, many volunteers, both youth and adult, have given their time to help out SVCU. This awards ceremony is a token of our appreciation for those people. Over twenty people attended this event, including San Jose councilmember Chappie Jones, who presented the awards. We thank all of the volunteers who have supported SVCU in the past year and we look forward to another year of service!
