Who We Are

The specific purpose of this Corporation shall be to organize and support community services opportunities, and to facilitate community unification and communication.

What We Do

Voter Registration

We do regular voter registration events to help new citizens in Silicon Valley become voters. Youth are highly encouraged to help out as they can get community service hours and learn about the processes of a government.

Summer Camps

We organize many summer camps, including volleyball and coding. Kids can come and learn new skills while having fun. The camps usually last a week and are held at local parks or other community areas.

Lectures

We host many lectures on various different topics. These topics can range from advice for college to how to get a job in the health field. The lectures also include a time where you can ask questions to the speaker.
